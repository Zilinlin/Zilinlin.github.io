Zilin Shen

申子琳 | PhD Candidate @ Purdue CS | Founding Engineer @ Eigen AI

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Purdue University, advised by Prof. Elisa Bertino. My research focuses on protocol security, formal verification, and LLM security.

I am also a Founding Engineer at Eigen AI, where I work on LLM infrastructure, including serving optimization, CUDA kernel development, and production deployment.

Previously, I received my B.E. from Tsinghua University in 2022.

Experience

  • Founding Engineer, Eigen AI, Aug 2024 - Present
    • LLM serving infrastructure and optimization
    • CUDA kernel development (FP4 Attention for B200)
    • Production deployment with Kubernetes and Kong Gateway
  • Network Security Research Intern, Cisco, Summer 2024

Research Interests

  • AI Security: LLM safety, protocol security (MCP), formal verification
  • Network Security: Wireless protocol analysis, fuzzing
